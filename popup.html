About AI Companion

AI Companion is your intelligent browsing assistant that provides contextual help and insights while you browse the web.

Features:

  • Real-time scroll detection and response
  • Floating avatar with drag-and-drop positioning
  • Smart contextual responses
  • Privacy-focused local processing

Privacy: All processing happens locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers.
